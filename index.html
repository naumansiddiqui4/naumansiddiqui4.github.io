ABOUT

I’m a Lead Engineer and founding team member at Classranked, where I design and build the backend systems that power our multi-tenant SaaS platform. I work across architecture, APIs, data modeling, security, microservices, and cloud infrastructure, and I lead engineering initiatives from planning through execution. Recently, I’ve also focused on bringing AI/LLMs into our reporting tools to help institutions explore their evaluation data more deeply and interactively.

Before stepping into a lead role, I helped grow ClassRanked from its early stages—modernizing our backend, building multi-tenant capabilities, developing automated ingestion workflows, improving performance, and creating internal tools that enabled us to scale rapidly across dozens of universities.

I completed my Master’s in Computer Science at Old Dominion University, where I worked in the Web Science & Digital Libraries (WS-DL) Lab under Dr. Michael L. Nelson and Dr. Michele C. Weigle. My research focused on using web archives to recover and analyze deleted tweets from members of the U.S. Congress, combining information retrieval, temporal analysis, and large-scale visualization. This work deepened my interest in systems that organize, preserve, and reason about complex data.

Prior to graduate school, I spent 2.5 years at Harman Connected Services (India), developing IoT Gateway components, wearable application integrations, and system-level Android/AOSP features for clients such as Vodafone, Verizon, The Weather Channel, AT&T, and British Telecom.

I’m passionate about system architecture, distributed services, secure and scalable data systems, and applying AI to real-world workflows.

If you’d like to connect or collaborate, feel free to reach out—I always enjoy meeting people who are building thoughtful, high-impact technology.
